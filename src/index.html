Welcome!

This is Anpang's Powder Simulator.

You are given a 320x320 square canvas where you can place things like dirt, sand, water, etc. Those tiles follow physics, so they will fall down due to gravity.

The controls are simple: click to place tiles and arrow keys to move. Hold shift to place more tiles or move more. This game currently does not work well on mobile!

This game is very new and experimental, and it's also open source. If you find bugs, please report them here.

Click anywhere to start!
